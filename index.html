What is it?

"Foursquare PreCheck-In allows people to express their intent to visit places before they even go. That can make it easier to plan trips with friends, and it can allow businesses to offer up services and products to potential customers in advance to help encourage them to actually go through with their visit and deliver better customer service." TechCrunch

Watch the presentation

PreCheck-In won the TechCrunch Disrupt Hackathon Europe 2013 in Berlin. This was the first hackathon of TechCrunch to take place in Europe.
